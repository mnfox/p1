About Me

Hi there! My name is Mithila Fox, and I am currently enrolled in the HES ALM IT program, focusing on Digital Media & Instructional Design. I have two undergrad degrees; one in Computer Science (majoring in Human-Computer Interaction), and one in Psychology (majoring in Cognitive Psychology and Neuroscience). I'm taking this course to broaden my understanding of back end development, as most of my experience is in front end dev. I have a fair amount of experience with HTML5, CSS, JS, Java, C#, and Python, and a little bit of experience with PHP, MySQL, and C. I'm currently running Mac OSX 10.9 Mavericks. And that's about it, really!
